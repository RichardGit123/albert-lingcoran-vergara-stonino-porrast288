Our Mission

The Bukidnon Medicinal Plants project seeks to preserve, document, and share the rich tradition of herbal knowledge in the Bukidnon region. We aim to support local healthcare and celebrate indigenous wisdom by providing accessible information about medicinal plants.

Who We Are

We are a team of students passionate about local botany, health, and the conservation of cultural heritage. Our work is inspired by the families, healers, and student researchers who have passed down their botanical knowledge through generations.
