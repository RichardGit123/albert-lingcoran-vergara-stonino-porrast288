Mango
(Mangifera indica)

About Mango Plant

Mango is a popular fruit with medicinal uses in Bukidnon for its vitamins and antioxidant properties. Traditionally, the leaves, inner bark, and fruit parts are utilized to boost immunity and support digestion.

Medicinal Uses

  • Boosts immune system with high vitamin C content
  • Supports digestive health and relieves constipation
  • Used for skin healing and anti-inflammatory effects

Preparation & Dosage

The fruit is eaten fresh or made into juice. Leaves are chewed or brewed into teas for medicinal purposes. Typical dosage varies with preparation, generally 1-2 cups of tea.
