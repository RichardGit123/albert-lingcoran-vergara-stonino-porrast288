Pansit-Pansitan
(Peperomia pellucida)

About Pansit-Pansitan

Pansit-Pansitan (also known as Ulasimang Bato) is a fleshy, heart-shaped herb that grows wild in the cool, damp areas of Bukidnon. It is one of the 10 medicinal plants approved by the Philippine Department of Health (DOH), specifically for lowering uric acid levels.

Medicinal Uses

  • Lowers Uric Acid: Clinically proven effective for treating gout and rheumatism.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces joint pain and swelling.
  • Analgesic: Provides natural pain relief.

Preparation & Dosage

Salad: Wash fresh leaves thoroughly and eat raw (1 cup daily) as a salad side dish.
Tea: Boil 1 cup of clean leaves in 2 cups of water for 15 minutes (do not cover the pot). Drink 1/2 cup 3 times a day.
