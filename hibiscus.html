Hibiscus
(Hibiscus rosa-sinensis)

About Hibiscus Plant

Hibiscus is a vibrant flowering plant used widely in traditional medicine for its cooling, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties. It is popularly used to treat fever, high blood pressure, and digestive issues in Bukidnon.

Medicinal Uses

  • Helps lower blood pressure and cholesterol
  • Relieves fever and reduces inflammation
  • Promotes healthy digestion and liver function

Preparation & Dosage

Hibiscus flowers and leaves are brewed into tea or applied topically to reduce inflammation. Recommended dosage is 1-2 cups of hibiscus tea daily.
