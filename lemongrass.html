Lemongrass
(Andropogon citratus)

About Lemongrass Plant

Lemongrass is widely used in Bukidnon as a herbal remedy for colds, fever, and digestive problems. Its leaves and stalks are boiled as tea or added to food for health benefits. Traditionally, it is valued for its antibacterial and aromatic properties.

Medicinal Uses

  • Relieves fever and mild colds
  • Helps digestion and reduces bloating
  • Used as a topical wash for wounds and skin issues

Preparation & Dosage

Lemongrass is often boiled and served as a tea. For external use, the liquid extract can be cooled and applied to the skin.
