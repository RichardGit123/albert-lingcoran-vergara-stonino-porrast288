Guava
(Psidium guajava)

About Guava Plant

Guava is a tropical fruit widely used in Bukidnon for its medicinal properties. It is valued for its high vitamin C content and antimicrobial activities. The leaves, bark, and fruit are traditionally used to treat digestive issues and infections.

Medicinal Uses

  • Relieves diarrhea and dysentery
  • Helps lower blood sugar levels
  • Used as a wound cleanser due to antibacterial properties

Preparation & Dosage

Guava leaves are boiled and consumed as tea to alleviate stomach ailments. The fruit is eaten fresh or used in juices as a dietary supplement.
