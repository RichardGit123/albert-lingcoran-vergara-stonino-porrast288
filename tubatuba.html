Tuba-tuba
(Jatropha curcas)

About Tuba-tuba Plant

Tuba-tuba, known scientifically as Jatropha curcas, is a plant used traditionally in Bukidnon for its medicinal and pesticidal properties. Though its seeds are toxic if ingested, parts of the plant are used externally for skin conditions and to repel insects.

Medicinal Uses

  • Used as an anti-inflammatory for skin irritations
  • Applied topically for wound healing and cuts
  • Used as a natural pesticide and insect repellent

Preparation & Dosage

Extracts from leaves or seeds are crushed and applied externally. Internal use is not recommended due to toxicity. Careful preparation ensures safety and efficacy.
