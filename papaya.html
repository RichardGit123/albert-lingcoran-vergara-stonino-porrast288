Papaya
(Carica papaya)

About Papaya Plant

Papaya is a common tropical fruit with multiple medicinal benefits. Locally used in Bukidnon to aid digestion, boost immunity, and treat skin disorders. The enzymes in the fruit and leaves promote healing.

Medicinal Uses

  • Improves digestion and relieves constipation
  • Supports wound healing and skin health
  • Used as an anti-inflammatory agent

Preparation & Dosage

Papaya fruit is eaten fresh or juiced, while leaves are boiled for medicinal teas. Topical application of leaf extracts also helps in wound care.
