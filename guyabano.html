Guyabano
(Annona muricata)

About Guyabano

Guyabano (Soursop) is a common fruit tree in Bukidnon backyards. While the fruit is delicious, the leaves are revered for their potent medicinal properties. Research suggests it contains acetogenins, natural compounds that are being studied for their health benefits.

Medicinal Uses

  • Hypertension: Traditionally used to help lower blood pressure.
  • Immune Booster: High vitamin C and antioxidant content strengthens the body.
  • Sedative: The leaf tea promotes better sleep and relaxation.

Preparation & Dosage

Tea: Clean 5-7 dried or fresh leaves. Boil in 1 liter of water until the water is reduced to half (approx. 500ml). Drink 1 cup in the morning and 1 cup in the evening.
