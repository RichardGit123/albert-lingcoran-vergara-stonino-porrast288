Ginger
(Zingiber officinale)

About Ginger Plant

Ginger is widely valued in Bukidnon herbal medicine for its anti-inflammatory and digestive benefits. The rhizome is used to treat nausea, colds, and muscular pain.

Medicinal Uses

  • Relieves nausea and motion sickness
  • Reduces inflammation and muscle pain
  • Supports respiratory health and cold relief

Preparation & Dosage

Ginger roots are sliced or crushed and brewed in hot water as tea, or used as supplements. Typical dosage is 1-2 grams daily for digestion support.
