Blumea
(Blumea balsamifera)

About Blumea Plant

Blumea, locally known and widely used in herbal medicine, is celebrated for its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The leaves and aerial parts have been traditionally applied for pain relief and wound treatment.

Medicinal Uses

  • Soothes skin inflammations and irritations
  • Treats respiratory ailments and cough
  • Used for wound healing and infection prevention

Preparation & Dosage

Blumea leaves are typically crushed and applied topically or boiled into decoctions for treating colds and skin conditions.
