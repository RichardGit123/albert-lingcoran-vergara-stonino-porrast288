Lagundi
(Vitex negundo)

About Lagundi Plant

Lagundi is perhaps the most extensively studied medicinal plant in the Philippines, scientifically proven to treat respiratory ailments. It is a shrub widely found in Bukidnon and has been commercialized into cough syrups and tablets due to its potent anti-asthma properties.

Medicinal Uses

  • Highly effective for relieving coughs, colds, and asthma
  • Proven bronchodilating effects (opens airways)
  • Used to treat fever and flu symptoms due to analgesic properties

Preparation & Dosage

Leaves are boiled in water (decoction) and drunk as tea. For cough and asthma, a half-cup of the decoction is typically taken three times a day. Commercially prepared capsules and syrups are also available.
