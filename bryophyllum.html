Bryophyllum
(Bryophyllum pinnatum)

About Bryophyllum Plant

Bryophyllum, also known as the "Miracle Leaf," is widely regarded for its healing and regenerative properties. It is commonly used in Bukidnon for wound healing, skin infections, and inflammation relief.

Medicinal Uses

  • Promotes wound healing and tissue regeneration
  • Reduces inflammation and soothes skin irritations
  • Used to relieve pain from insect bites and stings

Preparation & Dosage

Fresh leaves are crushed and applied topically on wounds or skin irritations. Decoctions may also be used for treating internal infections.
